The main event

Shakshuka for 9.

A deeply tomatoey, gently spicy pan with soft eggs and enough bread for enthusiastic mopping.

Cooking for
9
Planned table size
SHOPPING LIST

    For more than 6 people, use two wide pans. Crowded eggs are grumpy eggs.

    1

    Build the base

    Soften onions and peppers in olive oil for 10 minutes. Add garlic, cumin, paprika and chilli; let everything get fragrant.

    2

    Make it saucy

    Add tomatoes, season generously and simmer 15–20 minutes until thick enough to hold a little egg crater.

    3

    Drop the eggs

    Make wells, crack in the eggs, cover and cook 6–9 minutes. Stop while the yolks still have a promising wobble.

    4

    Finish with flourish

    Scatter feta, herbs and black pepper. Put the whole glorious pan on the table with warm bread.
